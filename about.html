About City Church International

City Church International, formly Evangel West Christian Life Center, was founded and estalisbed by Pastor Larry D. Coleman in 1999. CCI is a Word of God Standard Ministry and a non denominational church. Each member is encouraged to live New Testament christian lives through prayer, daily communion, fasting, and believing in all nine gifts of the Holy Spirt found in 1 Corinthians 12.

Pastors Larry D. & Denise Coleman


Pastor Larry D. Coleman attended Southern Baptist Boyce Bible College for four years. He severed on the board of Prodigal Ministries as Program Coordinator; he also served on staff as an ordained Minister with the Evangel World Prayer. Pastor Coleman was the Executive Director of the Lords Kitchen and founded the prison ministry called Reconciliation Ministries. He is also the Founder and President of Feed The City, Inc, a non profit organization that feeds and clothes needy families of Louisville and Southern Indiana.

Pastor Denise Coleman radiates joy and has dedicated her life to be the catalyst that helps others transform thier lives and realize the destiny that God has for them. She functions in every area of the church including preaching, teaching, and leading worship. She is the President and Founder of Women of Destiny Ministry.

Pastors Larry and Denise have been married for nearly 20 years, and God has blessed them with beautiful children and grandchildren.
